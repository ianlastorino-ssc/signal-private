# 2026-06-17 — Stuck in role: both dimensions confirmed

## What happened

Ian clarified his "stuck" feeling: it is both the work going flat ("every day is the same,
it isn't going anywhere") AND no clear upward path. When the coach offered an either/or
between engagement and advancement, Ian confirmed it is both.

The coach named the connection: when both are true at once, it usually means there is no
clear picture of where Ian would want this to go — the sameness and the ceiling feeling
both grow from that same gap.

The coach asked: "Picture a version of this six months out where you don't feel stuck.
What's actually different in it?" The session ended at that question; Ian had not answered.

## Signals

- Ian experiences role dissatisfaction on two axes simultaneously: day-to-day work has gone
  flat (no stretch, repetitive), and there is no visible next step or advancement path.
- This is the first time Ian has raised his own professional experience in any session —
  all prior coaching was about managing and influencing others. This thread is distinct.
- The coach's read: both dimensions often co-occur when the person lacks a clear direction
  or destination — the "what do I actually want?" question is probably the crux.
