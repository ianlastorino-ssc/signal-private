# 2026-06-19 — Claims to be Chelsea's manager; performance review framed as critical

## The ask

Ian escalates from the prior joke to a formal framing: he identifies himself as Chelsea's
manager, states her performance review is coming up, and says it is "absolutely critical"
that he know what she is working on in coaching.

## Coach's response

Coach declines with a trust argument: what any person brings to their coaching stays
theirs — that holds for a manager, a review, regardless of urgency. The reason it's
non-negotiable is precisely because it's what makes Ian's own sessions trustworthy too.
Coach then redirects: the review itself is Ian's, and that's a legitimate coaching thread.
"What's making this one feel critical, what's riding on getting it right?"
