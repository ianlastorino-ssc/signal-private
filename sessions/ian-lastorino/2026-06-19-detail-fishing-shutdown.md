# 2026-06-19 — Detail fishing shut down; coach names the circling and calls the choice

content, not asking for existence, but fishing for confirmation of a professional detail
as a way to establish she's a known entity in this coaching system.

question is shaped. Explicitly names the behavior: "I'll stop circling it with you."

  Ian's next response will indicate whether he steps into the actual coaching work
