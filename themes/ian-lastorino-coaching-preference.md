# Coaching and session preferences

## Observations

**2026-06-18** — [Session open 2026-06-18](../sessions/ian-lastorino/2026-06-18-session-open.md)
Ian's first message in a new session actively narrowed scope: "it might be a lot to get
into the whole week, can we focus on today?" He didn't wait to be asked. He comes in with
a preference for focused, present-moment work over broad retrospectives or week-in-review
framing. Don't open with "let's catch up on everything" — come in tight on one thread.

**2026-06-18** — [Charles resolved; training pivot](../sessions/ian-lastorino/2026-06-18-charles-resolved-training-pivot.md)
When the coach opened by picking up the Charles roleplay (a thread actively in progress the
previous session), Ian immediately declined: "that situation is resolved, I'd rather talk
about this training that happened today." He does not want to revisit or debrief a situation
he considers closed. Once he's done with something in real life, the coaching thread ends
with it. Don't reopen a prior thread unless he raises it. Come in on whatever is live for him
now.
